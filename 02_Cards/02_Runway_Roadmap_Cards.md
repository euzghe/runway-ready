# Deck 2 — Runway Roadmap Cards (×12)

**ISO/IEC 29119-3 mapping:** Project Test Plan
**ISO fields preserved on every card:** *Scope · References · Schedule · Resources · Risks · Approach*
**Function in play:** Defines the round-specific constraints. Every Theme and Outfit played this round must satisfy the active Roadmap.

---

## RR-01 — "SS26 Capsule Drop"

- *Scope:* Up to 4 Outfits, day-wear only
- *References:* Active Brand Identity
- *Schedule:* 1 round (10 min)
- *Resources:* 1 accessory cap per Outfit
- *Risks:* Tight schedule, weather-sensitive fabrics
- *Approach:* Fast-iteration, single rehearsal

## RR-02 — "Couture Gala"

- *Scope:* Exactly 6 Outfits, evening-wear
- *References:* Brand Identity + Heritage clause if applicable
- *Schedule:* 2 rounds
- *Resources:* Unlimited accessories; max 2 hand-finished elements per Outfit
- *Risks:* Cost overruns, fitter fatigue
- *Approach:* Heavy specification, dual rehearsal

## RR-03 — "Resort Pop-Up"

- *Scope:* 3–5 Outfits, beach/resort
- *References:* Active Brand Identity
- *Schedule:* 1 round
- *Resources:* No fragile materials
- *Risks:* Sand, sun, salt-water exposure
- *Approach:* Lightweight, durable fabrics

## RR-04 — "Industry Press Show"

- *Scope:* 4 Outfits, press-friendly
- *References:* Brand Identity + press-kit reference
- *Schedule:* 1 round, +30 sec inspection
- *Resources:* 2 accessory cap
- *Risks:* Live photography, journalists' scrutiny
- *Approach:* Each Outfit must have a unique selling point

## RR-05 — "Student Showcase"

- *Scope:* 3 Outfits, mixed casual / formal
- *References:* Brand Identity
- *Schedule:* 1 round
- *Resources:* Tight budget — max 1 hand-finished element across all Outfits
- *Risks:* Inexperienced models
- *Approach:* Forgiving silhouettes

## RR-06 — "Fashion Week Finale"

- *Scope:* 5 Outfits, high-impact
- *References:* Brand Identity + signature piece reference
- *Schedule:* 2 rounds
- *Resources:* Unlimited
- *Risks:* Press backlash, wardrobe fatigue
- *Approach:* Crescendo — final Outfit must be the standout

## RR-07 — "Sustainability Showcase"

- *Scope:* 4 Outfits, certified materials only
- *References:* Brand Identity (must be sustainability-aligned)
- *Schedule:* 1 round
- *Resources:* No leather, no synthetic fur
- *Risks:* Material sourcing failures
- *Approach:* Auditor visit possible

## RR-08 — "Bridal Collection"

- *Scope:* 4 Outfits, bridal only
- *References:* Brand Identity + heritage code if applicable
- *Schedule:* 2 rounds
- *Resources:* 3 hand-finished elements minimum per Outfit
- *Risks:* Symbolic detail mismatches
- *Approach:* Slow, traceable revisions

## RR-09 — "Streetwear Drop"

- *Scope:* 5 Outfits, casual urban
- *References:* Brand Identity
- *Schedule:* 1 round
- *Resources:* No couture techniques
- *Risks:* Trend-cycle mismatch
- *Approach:* Fast turnaround

## RR-10 — "Pre-Fall Lookbook"

- *Scope:* 4 Outfits, layered
- *References:* Brand Identity
- *Schedule:* 1 round
- *Resources:* Outerwear required on every Outfit
- *Risks:* Studio overheating
- *Approach:* Static photography rehearsal

## RR-11 — "Avant-Garde Concept Show"

- *Scope:* 3 Outfits, conceptual
- *References:* Brand Identity (Kuro & Co. recommended)
- *Schedule:* 1 round
- *Resources:* Unlimited; one Outfit *must* break a rule (artist's statement)
- *Risks:* Press misunderstanding
- *Approach:* High-risk, high-reward

## RR-12 — "Inclusive Sizing Showcase"

- *Scope:* 4 Outfits across ≥3 distinct sizes
- *References:* Brand Identity (Maison Aurore aligned)
- *Schedule:* 1 round
- *Resources:* Tailoring time prioritised
- *Risks:* Sizing mismatches
- *Approach:* Each Outfit must declare its size in Pre-conditions

---

## Why these matter (educational intent)

A Roadmap operationalises the Brand Identity for a specific round — exactly as a Project Test Plan operationalises the Organizational Test Strategy for a specific project. A learner who plays *Couture Gala* but writes only 3 Outfits has produced a **scope violation** — a real ISO defect class. Inspection will catch it.
